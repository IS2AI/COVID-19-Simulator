This is the dataset for preliminary simulations for COVID-19 spread in Kazakhstan from 1st March 2020 untill 14th April 2020. 
The dataset can be loaded to the simulator to analyze the potential conditions after 14th April 2020.

1.Unzip the files and put to the *streaming/results/* directory of the simulator.
2. Type the name of the folder to the simulator and press "Load data from directory results/"
